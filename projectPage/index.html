Interiors
Inspired by the soothing colours and textures of the surrounding environment and the lake views , as well as the historical roots of the town, the interiors at The Ann honour the natural landscape of Port Credit, while forging ahead with a more refined aesthetic for today’s modern lifestyle.
Echoing the Gentle Hues of Nature
Suites range in size from the XXX-square-foot one-bedroom plan, to XXXX-square-foot three-bedroom layouts, all with spacious private balconies and far-reaching views across the park, the city, or the lake through floor–to–ceiling glass windows.

The design embodies a blend of timeless aesthetics, simplicity, practicality, and conscientious detailing. In the kitchen, quartz countertops and backsplash, metal accents, soft-close drawers, and integrated appliances create an inviting and inspiring space. In the bathroom, innovative designs like wood accents, travertine-inspired tiles, and a custom medicine cabinet, balance form and function. Optimized space and storage prioritize convenience, and finishes throughout echo the gentle hues of nature.
An Eye to Sustainability
Sustainability underpins the overall design at The Ann, with thoughtful features and practices that support a more environ-mentally-responsible and sustainable future.

Materials like eco-friendly flooring, bird-friendly glazing, and green-rated finishes contribute to a healthier environment for both residents and the planet. Signature natural wood elements appear throughout, adding a sense of warmth. And everywhere, thoughtful touches enhance the residential experience, like the background of greenery in the lobby, creating an oasis of calm.
